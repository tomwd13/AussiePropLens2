FAQ

Questions answered

Is there a free trial?

The residential analyser is free to use with no credit card required. Commercial and institutional tiers offer a 7-day free trial.

How does postcode lookup work?

Type any 4-digit Australian postcode and suburb data loads instantly — no searching or free typing required. Covers all major metros and regional centres.

How accurate is the AI analysis?

AI insights are generated using Claude by Anthropic, contextualised to your specific property inputs and suburb data. They are designed to inform, not replace, professional due diligence.

What are the AI call limits?

Residential plans get 1,000 AI calls per month. Commercial plans get 5,000. Institutional plans are unlimited. Limits reset on the 1st of each month.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. No lock-in contracts, no cancellation fees. Cancel from your account dashboard any time.

Is this financial advice?

No. AussiePropLens is an analysis and information tool. It does not constitute financial, legal, or investment advice. Always consult a licensed professional before making investment decisions.
